Welcome to Nionjord Investments

Nionjord Investments is a boutique investment firm incorporated and based in The Hague, Netherlands, operating as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Lone Cypress Holdings. The company functions as an in-house investment vehicle with the primary objective of deploying excess capital generated by Lone Cypress Holdings and its affiliated subsidiaries into diversified market investments to generate positive returns. Nionjord Investments conducts its trading activities exclusively through Interactive Brokers as its designated investment platform, limiting its investment scope to listed non-derivative financial instruments, specifically stocks and exchange-traded funds (ETFs). The firm's investment mandate encompasses securities markets in the United States, Canada, the European Union (including the United Kingdom), Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, Australia, and New Zealand, operating within the regulatory frameworks governing investment activities in the Netherlands and applicable international securities regulations.
