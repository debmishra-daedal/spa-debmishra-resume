Data Retention

Your personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes it was collected, in compliance with our data retention policies and regulations.

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and restrict the processing of your personal data. You also have the right to data portability and to object to processing.

We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties. However, we may share data with trusted third parties for the purposes outlined in this policy, under strict data processing agreements.

Security Measures

We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
